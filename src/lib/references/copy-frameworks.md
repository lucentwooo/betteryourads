# Copy Frameworks Reference

Proven formulas for high-converting ad copy.

---

## 1. PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

**Structure:**
1. **Problem:** Call out specific pain
2. **Agitate:** Amplify emotional cost
3. **Solution:** Your product as relief

**Example (Problem-Aware Stage):**
- Headline: "Tired of chasing late invoices?"
- Agitate: "Every 'I'll pay next week' costs you hours of stress and cash flow headaches."
- Solution: "FreelanceFlow auto-reminds clients so you don't have to."

**Best For:** Cold audiences who don't know they need you yet

---

## 2. AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

**Structure:**
1. **Attention:** Pattern interrupt
2. **Interest:** Relatable context
3. **Desire:** Benefits + proof
4. **Action:** Clear CTA

**Example (Product-Aware Stage):**
- Attention: "This saved me 5 hours last week"
- Interest: "As a freelancer, invoicing used to eat my Sundays..."
- Desire: "Now it runs on autopilot. 10,000+ freelancers use it daily."
- Action: "Try it free for 14 days"

**Best For:** Moving warm audiences to trial

---

## 3. BFB (Before-After-Bridge)

**Structure:**
1. **Before:** Current struggle
2. **After:** Dream outcome
3. **Bridge:** How to get there (your product)

**Example (Solution-Aware Stage):**
- Before: "Invoicing chaos → Late nights → Unpaid bills"
- After: "Auto-invoicing → Paid on time → Weekends back"
- Bridge: "FreelanceFlow makes it happen in 3 clicks."

**Best For:** Solution-aware audiences comparing options

---

## 4. The 4U Framework

**Requirements:**
- **Urgent:** Time pressure
- **Unique:** Specific mechanism
- **Useful:** Clear benefit
- **Ultra-specific:** Concrete numbers

**Example (Most-Aware Stage):**
- "Get paid 23% faster (in 14 days or less) with this invoice automation used by 10,000+ freelancers"

**Best For:** Retargeting and high-intent audiences

---

## 5. Story-First Hook

**Rules:**
- Start mid-action (not "Once upon a time")
- Show, don't tell
- End with CTA

**Example (Problem-Aware):**
- "I almost quit freelancing last Tuesday. Not because of bad clients—because I spent 6 hours on invoices."

**Best For:** UGC-style ads, relatable founder stories

---

## 6. The Curiosity Gap

**Structure:**
- Open with something unexpected
- Withhold the full answer
- CTA promises resolution

**Example (Problem-Aware):**
- "I made $40K last month. Here's what I stopped doing..."

**Best For:** Stopping the scroll on cold traffic

---

## 7. The Direct Callout

**Structure:**
- "[Audience] who [specific situation]..."
- Present solution
- CTA

**Example (Solution-Aware):**
- "Freelancers who still invoice in Google Sheets—there's a better way."

**Best For:** Niche audiences who self-identify

---

## Framework by Awareness Stage

| Stage | Primary Frameworks |
|-------|-------------------|
| Problem-aware | PAS, Curiosity Gap, Direct Callout |
| Solution-aware | BFB, Direct Callout, Story-First |
| Product-aware | AIDA, Testimonial, Social Proof |
| Most-aware | 4U, Urgency, Offer-focused |

---

## Hook Templates

**Problem-Aware Hooks:**
- "Tired of [specific pain]?"
- "Still doing [old way] in [current year]?"
- "Every [audience] faces this..."
- "I almost quit [thing] because of this..."

**Solution-Aware Hooks:**
- "What if you could [benefit] in [timeframe]?"
- "The [audience] secret to [outcome]"
- "Stop [old way]. Start [new way]."

**Product-Aware Hooks:**
- "10,000+ [audience] use this to [benefit]"
- "This [product] saved me [specific result]"
- "Finally, a [product] that actually [works/delivers]"

**Most-Aware Hooks:**
- "Last chance: [offer] ends [deadline]"
- "Get [benefit]—[discount] off today only"
- "The [product] [specific audience] swear by"

---

## CTA Templates

**Low Friction:**
- "See how it works →"
- "Learn more"
- "Swipe up to try"
- "Tap to claim"

**Medium Commitment:**
- "Get started free"
- "Try it for 14 days"
- "Join [X] others"

**High Intent:**
- "Start your free trial"
- "Get [X]% off now"
- "Book your demo"
